Privacy Policy for Audiobookify

Last updated: June 19, 2026
Audiobookify is designed as a private EPUB reader and listen-mode audiobook app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.
## Information We Collect
Audiobookify does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal information directly from you.
The app may store the following information locally on your device so the reader and listen mode can function:
Imported EPUB files
Book metadata from imported EPUBs, such as title, author, cover image, and book identifier
Reading progress, including chapter position, scroll position, and listen-mode sentence position
Reader settings and preferences
Text-to-speech settings, voices, text replacement rules, and playback preferences
Downloaded text-to-speech model and voice files if you enable neural voices
Local EPUB Storage
When you import an EPUB from Files, from another app, or from a generated download, Audiobookify copies the book into its local app storage so it can appear in your library and reopen later.
Audiobookify also stores extracted reading files in the device cache while you read. Cache files may be removed by the system or by deleting the app.
## iCloud Sync
If you enable iCloud Sync, Audiobookify may store reader data in your personal iCloud account so it can be backed up or restored across your devices.
This may include:
Reading progress
Listen-mode sentence position
Reader settings
iCloud data is stored through Apple's iCloud services and is associated with your Apple ID. Audiobookify does not operate its own server for iCloud Sync and does not have access to your Apple ID password.
## Fanfiction URL Import
Audiobookify includes an optional Fanfiction URL import feature powered by FicHub.
If you paste a story URL and choose to fetch or download it, Audiobookify sends that URL to FicHub so FicHub can return story metadata and generate or provide an EPUB download. FicHub may handle that request according to its own privacy practices.
Audiobookify does not control FicHub and is not responsible for FicHub's content, availability, data handling, or privacy practices.
## Text-to-Speech and Model Downloads
Audiobookify includes listen mode using Apple voices and optional on-device neural text-to-speech engines.
Book text is processed for speech on your device by the selected engine. Audiobookify does not send your book text to an Audiobookify server.
If you download optional neural voice models or voice files, the app may connect to model download services used by the text-to-speech engine. Those services may receive standard network request information such as your IP address and user agent. Downloaded models and voice files are stored locally on your device and can be removed from the app's text-to-speech settings.
## Web Content and External Links
Audiobookify renders EPUB chapters with Apple's WebKit technology from files stored on your device.
EPUB books may contain links or references to external websites. If you open third-party websites or content, those sites may collect information according to their own privacy policies.
Audiobookify does not control third-party websites and is not responsible for their content, tracking, cookies, analytics, or privacy practices.
## Analytics and Tracking
Audiobookify does not include third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or cross-app tracking.
The app does not sell your personal information.
## Data Sharing
Audiobookify does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.
Data may be handled by Apple services when you use iCloud, TestFlight, crash reporting, App Store distribution, or other Apple platform features, subject to Apple's privacy policies.
Data may also be handled by FicHub if you choose to use Fanfiction URL import, and by model download services if you choose to download optional neural text-to-speech models.
## Children's Privacy
Audiobookify is not directed at children under 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
## Your Choices
You can remove locally stored library data by deleting books from Audiobookify or by deleting the app from your device.
You can manage iCloud data through Audiobookify's iCloud Sync settings and through your device's iCloud settings.
You can remove downloaded neural text-to-speech models and voices from Audiobookify's text-to-speech settings.
Changes to This Privacy Policy
This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted with a new "Last updated" date.
